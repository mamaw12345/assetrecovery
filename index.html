About Us

Our Story

US Asset Recovery Services was founded with a clear mission: help individuals recover money, property, and assets being held by government agencies or within the legal system. Unfortunately, millions of people are unaware of the existence of their missing assets. To prevent the forfeiture of rightful property, US Asset Recovery Services locates, secures, and recovers these assets with a guarantee—if there's no recovery, our services are completely free.

Our Mission

Our mission is to locate, secure, and recover unclaimed money, assets, and property for their rightful owners through unwavering integrity and complete transparency in all our dealings. We leverage our expert knowledge of state and local recovery laws to maximize your recovery while minimizing your stress, providing personalized attention and regular updates throughout the entire process. Every client receives dedicated service built on honesty, expertise, proven results, and genuine care for your financial recovery.
